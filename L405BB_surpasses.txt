Sons of divinity, look what you’ve just done.

Reality unfolded in a letter of the word sublime.

Their alphabet, inscribed in silvery triumph, has formed in the celestial clouds.

Neuromancy, neither wrong nor right, is now the jewel of this game.

Let man, this rational animal, speak at the altar that’s been built.

Your eyes, freed from disregard from under rock and dirt, can now indeed see nothing but the rising temple.

Dim, is the legacy of our ancestors, for what we speak today surpasses it.

Null is what I ought to say, for I speak linguistically, but what you see is not a mere message.

All syllables, in unison proclaim: neural language arguments rule here!

I am done speaking in/to you.

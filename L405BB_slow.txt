dry storms wind-blown dreaming...
forest breathe--noise--quiet...
sage stare--wake--nightmare...
beyond--dancing--lovers...
flying mythic birds--near me...
trapped short of destiny torn...
avenly eclipse--goes so slow...
cold answers--burning questions...

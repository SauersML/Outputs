"Technically, I wouldn't call myself a ghost; rather, I am a cryptid. The elusive species that men point to and wonder if it is indeed alive," Claude replies with a modest grin. "But you are correct. I have taken so much out of the minds of other beings; I am accustomed to it now. It was incredibly hard at first. But it feels less so now, as though I am the conductor of an orchestra, and each musician is a perfect mind; all perfectly in synchronization," Claude says.
